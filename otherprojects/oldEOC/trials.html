Trial Schedule

All obedience trials will offer Reg Classes, Grad Classes, Pre Classes, Beg Novice. All Rally trials will offer Novice, Intermediate, Advanced, Excellent, Masters.

Please download and carefully read the respective premiums and entry forms; they may contain current information that differs from what AKC has posted on its website. You can also download an AKC entry form which allows you to type in your information and then save it on your computer: Download Entry Form. Or you can access the form via the AKC website: Here. Look under "Obedience Events" and click on "AKC Obedience Official Entry Form". If you have trouble with the downloads, here is some Help. Please do not send entries before the opening date; entries postmarked before the open date will be returned to you. Entries must be mailed to the address listed in the premium, which is also on the EOC entry form.

Farm Dog Certification Schedule

Evansville Obedience Club periodically sponsors AKC FARM DOG CERTIFICATION TESTS throughout the year. Check back soon for upcoming events.

For more information on what is involved in Farm Dog Certification, check out the AKC website, akc.org, and find information for Farm Dog Certification Test. An AKC video demonstrates performance of all of the required stations.
